What is EcoCrypt.org?

Ecocrypt.org. is a social network that allows concerned citizens to report and resolve pollution with their appropriate authorities. Ecocrypt was first pitch as an idea while I was studying Environmental Science at Galen Univeristy, Belize. As the semesters went by, each class got more intense and adventuous, eventually taking us the the famous, beautiful, and immensely important wetland in belize - Crooked Tree Wild Life Sanctuary! We were schedule to stay at Crooked Tree Wild Life Sactuary for 2 nights, where we were expected to explore a day in the life of a water ecologist, but more so to explore opportunities on how to expand our careers.
